Driver, Mahindra racing

Jake Hughes brings over 10 years’ experience in single-seater competition to the NEOM McLaren Formula E Team. During this time, Jake became the inaugural BRDC Formula 4 champion. Then, he completed a two-year stint in several Formula Renault Championships, finishing second in Formula Renault 2.0 Alps in 2015.  The Brit then secured numerous Formula 3 race wins between 2016 and 2020 before graduating to Formula 2 for the 2022 season, splitting his time in the series with Formula 3. In addition to racing in Formula 2, he held a reserve and development driver role in Formula E during Season 7 and Season 8. Jake competed in Formula E on a full-time basis with NEOM McLaren in Season 9, where he secured two pole positions and a fifth-place finish in his first race. After impressing during this time, he will remain with the team for Season 10.
